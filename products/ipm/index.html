Fuji Electric IPM (Intelligent Power Modules)

Integrated power modules with built-in protection and control for motor drive applications

Fuji Electric's Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) integrate IGBTs or power MOSFETs with a drive circuit, protection circuit, and control circuit in a single package. This integration provides compact, efficient solutions for motor drive applications with enhanced reliability and simplified design.

Key Features

  • Integrated drive and protection circuits
  • Compact design for space-constrained applications
  • Built-in protection functions (over-current, over-temperature, under-voltage)
  • Optimized for variable speed motor control
  • Multiple package options for various applications

Applications

  • Industrial motor drives
  • Home appliances (air conditioners, washing machines)
  • Fans and pumps
  • Compressors
  • Electric power steering

Integrated Protection Features

Over-Current Protection (OCP)

Protects against over-current conditions that could damage the module

Over-Temperature Protection (OTP)

Monitors junction temperature and limits operation to prevent thermal damage

Under-Voltage Lockout (UVLO)

Prevents operation when supply voltage is below safe operating levels

Short-Circuit Protection (SCP)

Protects against output short circuits with fast detection and response
