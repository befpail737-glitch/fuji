2MBI150XAA-50

7th Generation X-Series IGBT Module

SKU: 2MBI150XAA-50

Voltage: 1200V
Current: 150A
Configuration: Dual IGBT
Series: 7th Generation X-Series
Request Quote Request Sample Download Datasheet

Product Description

The 2MBI150XAA-50 is a 7th Generation X-Series IGBT module from Fuji Electric, designed for high-efficiency power conversion applications. This dual IGBT module features advanced trench technology with field stop structure, delivering ultra-low switching and conduction losses.

With a higher current rating than the 2MBI100XAA-50, this module is ideal for applications requiring greater power handling capability while maintaining the same high efficiency standards.

Applications

  • Industrial motor drives (higher power)
  • Solar inverters (string inverters)
  • Uninterruptible power supplies (UPS)
  • Welding equipment (high power)
  • Industrial heating systems

FAE Technical Commentary

The 2MBI150XAA-50 offers an excellent balance of current capability and efficiency for mid-to-high power applications. The 7th Generation technology provides approximately 20% lower switching losses compared to the 6th Generation, making it ideal for applications operating at switching frequencies up to 20kHz.

When designing with this module, pay attention to gate drive requirements and thermal management to fully utilize its capabilities while ensuring reliable operation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can this module be used in parallel configurations?

Yes, this module is suitable for parallel operation due to its positive temperature coefficient and well-matched electrical characteristics.

What is the maximum operating temperature?

The maximum junction temperature is 175°C with storage temperature from -40°C to 150°C.

How does this compare to 6th Generation modules?

The 7th Generation offers approximately 20% lower switching losses and 10% lower conduction losses compared to the 6th Generation.
