2-Unit IGBT Modules

Half-bridge configurations for various applications

Fuji Electric's 2-unit IGBT modules integrate a complementary pair of IGBTs and diodes in a single package, forming a half-bridge or phase-leg configuration. These modules are ideal for a wide range of applications including DC/DC converters, single-phase inverters, and multi-level topologies.

Key Features

  • Complementary IGBT and diode pair
  • Multiple package options (TO-247, etc.)
  • Consistent electrical characteristics
  • Thermally optimized design
  • Available with 7th and 6th Generation chips

Applications

  • Half-bridge converters
  • Phase-leg topologies
  • Resonant converters
  • Single-phase inverters
  • Multi-level converters
