6-Unit IGBT Modules

Compact 3-phase inverter modules with integrated control

Fuji Electric's 6-unit IGBT modules integrate all six IGBTs and diodes required for a 3-phase inverter in a single compact package. These modules are ideal for industrial motor drives, UPS systems, and renewable energy applications.

Key Features

  • Complete 3-phase inverter in single package
  • Integrated gate resistors in some models
  • Low stray inductance design
  • Multiple voltage and current ratings
  • Compatible with 7th and 6th Generation IGBT chips

Available Ratings

  • Voltage: 1200V, 1700V, 3300V options
  • Current: 50A to 600A ratings
  • Package: Various industrial packages
