Fuji Electric IGBT Modules

Advanced IGBT technology for high-efficiency power conversion

Fuji Electric's IGBT modules represent the cutting edge of power semiconductor technology. Our 7th Generation X-Series offers improved efficiency and reliability, while the 6th Generation V-Series provides optimal performance for industrial applications. The PrimePACK™ series delivers high current density in a compact form factor.

Key Series

  • 7th Generation X-Series: Ultra-low loss technology for energy-efficient systems
  • 6th Generation V-Series: Optimized for industrial drives and renewable energy
  • PrimePACK™: High-current solutions for automotive and industrial applications

Applications

  • Industrial motor drives
  • Renewable energy systems (solar inverters, wind inverters)
  • Uninterruptible power supplies (UPS)
  • Electric vehicles and charging stations
  • Rail transportation systems
