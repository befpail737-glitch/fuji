Boost PIM Modules

Modules for power factor correction and boost converter applications

Fuji Electric's Boost PIM (Power Integrated Module) integrates the power devices needed for boost converter applications in a single compact package. This includes IGBTs and diodes optimized for power factor correction (PFC) and boost converter circuits.

Key Features

  • Integrated boost converter topology
  • Optimized for PFC applications
  • Space-saving design
  • Improved thermal performance
  • Integrated snubber circuits in some models

Applications

  • Active power factor correction
  • Boost converter circuits
  • DC/DC power supplies
  • LED drivers
  • Adapter applications
