2DI40-65A0

Power Integrated Module (PIM)

SKU: 2DI40-65A0

Voltage: 650V
Current: 40A
Configuration: Inverter PIM
Package: CIB 4-in-1
Request Quote Request Sample Download Datasheet

Product Description

The 2DI40-65A0 is a Power Integrated Module from Fuji Electric, combining IGBT and diode chips in a single compact package. This inverter PIM configuration is designed for 3-phase inverter applications, offering significant space savings and simplified assembly compared to discrete component solutions.

Part of the CIB (Converter IPM) series, this module integrates the full inverter bridge function in a single package, reducing design complexity and system size.

Applications

FAE Technical Commentary

The 2DI40-65A0 PIM offers significant advantages in designs where board space is at a premium. The integrated design eliminates many of the parasitic inductances that occur in discrete designs, leading to cleaner switching waveforms and reduced EMI.

When implementing this PIM in your design, ensure adequate thermal management as the power density in the package requires efficient heat transfer to the heatsink.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recommended heatsink for this PIM?

For continuous operation at rated current, a heatsink with thermal resistance of 0.5°C/W or lower is recommended.

Can I parallel multiple PIMs for higher power?

While technically possible, it's generally not recommended due to the integrated nature of the PIM. Consider a discrete solution for parallel applications.

What is the typical efficiency of systems using this PIM?

With proper design, systems can achieve >97% efficiency at rated conditions.
