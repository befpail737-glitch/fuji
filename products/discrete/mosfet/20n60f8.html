Product Description

The 20N60F8 is a high-performance 600V N-channel power MOSFET from Fuji Electric, designed for switching power supplies, DC/DC converters, and other high-frequency applications. This device features low on-resistance and fast switching characteristics.

Utilizing Fuji Electric's advanced silicon processing technology, this MOSFET delivers excellent performance in power conversion applications with reduced switching and conduction losses.

FAE Technical Commentary

The 20N60F8 is optimized for hard switching applications in the 600V range where low on-resistance is important for efficiency. The low RDS(on) of 0.25Ω helps reduce conduction losses, while the moderate gate charge allows for efficient switching at moderate frequencies (50-100kHz).

This device works well in bridge topologies and is particularly effective in continuous conduction mode PFC applications where the low forward voltage drop of the body diode is beneficial.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum operating frequency for this MOSFET?

While the device can operate at frequencies exceeding 100kHz, the recommended operating frequency is typically below 100kHz for optimal efficiency in hard switching applications.

What gate driver is recommended for this MOSFET?

Due to the gate charge of 45nC, a driver capable of sourcing and sinking at least 2A is recommended for efficient switching at high frequencies.

How does this compare to super-junction MOSFETs?

The 20N60F8 is a planar MOSFET with lower cost but higher RDS(on) compared to super-junction devices. It suits applications where cost is prioritized over maximum efficiency.
