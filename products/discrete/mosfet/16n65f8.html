16N65F8

650V N-Channel Power MOSFET

SKU: 16N65F8

Voltage: 650V
Current: 16A
Package: TO-247
Type: N-Channel
Request Quote Request Sample Download Datasheet

Product Description

The 16N65F8 is a high-performance 650V N-channel power MOSFET from Fuji Electric, designed for switching power supplies, DC/DC converters, and other high-frequency applications. This device features low on-resistance and fast switching characteristics.

With a higher voltage rating than the 600V series, this MOSFET is ideal for applications requiring extra voltage headroom or operation in 400V AC systems.

Technical Specifications

Parameter Symbol Value Unit
Drain-Source Voltage Vdss 650 V
Drain Current (DC) Id 16 A
Drain Current (Pulsed) Idm 64 A
On-Resistance Rds(on) 0.35 Ω
Gate Threshold Voltage Vgs(th) 2.5 V
Input Capacitance Ciss 1050 pF
Output Capacitance Coss 380 pF
Diode Forward Voltage Vsd 1.3 V
Total Gate Charge Qg 38 nC

Applications

  • Switching power supplies (400V AC input)
  • DC/DC converters
  • High-voltage PFC circuits
  • Motor control
  • LED lighting systems

FAE Technical Commentary

The 16N65F8 is optimized for applications requiring higher voltage ratings than standard 600V devices. While the RDS(on) is slightly higher than the 600V series due to the higher voltage rating, it still provides excellent performance in switching applications.

This device works well in bridge topologies and is particularly effective in continuous conduction mode PFC applications where the higher voltage rating provides additional design margin.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum operating frequency for this MOSFET?

While the device can operate at frequencies exceeding 100kHz, the recommended operating frequency is typically below 100kHz for optimal efficiency in hard switching applications.

What gate driver is recommended for this MOSFET?

Due to the gate charge of 38nC, a driver capable of sourcing and sinking at least 2A is recommended for efficient switching at high frequencies.

How does this compare to super-junction MOSFETs?

The 16N65F8 is a planar MOSFET with lower cost but higher RDS(on) compared to super-junction devices. It suits applications where cost is prioritized over maximum efficiency.
