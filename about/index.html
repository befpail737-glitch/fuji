About LiTong Group

Authorized distributor of Fuji Electric power semiconductors with global reach and technical expertise

Our Company

LiTong Group has been a trusted partner in the power semiconductor industry for over 15 years, serving as an authorized distributor for leading manufacturers including Fuji Electric. Our commitment to providing exceptional service and technical support has established us as a reliable partner for customers worldwide.

Founded in 2005, we have grown to become a comprehensive solution provider for power semiconductor needs, with expertise in IGBT modules, IPM, PIM, and discrete devices. Our focus on technical excellence and customer satisfaction has enabled us to build lasting relationships with customers across various industries.

Our Mission

To deliver the highest quality power semiconductor solutions with unparalleled technical support, enabling our customers to create innovative products that contribute to a sustainable and energy-efficient future.

Our Partnership with Fuji Electric

As an authorized distributor, LiTong Group maintains a strong partnership with Fuji Electric, one of the world's leading manufacturers of power semiconductors. This partnership ensures that we provide our customers with:

  • Access to the latest Fuji Electric products, including the 7th Generation X-Series IGBTs
  • Direct technical support from Fuji Electric's application engineers
  • Prioritized allocation during supply constraints
  • Latest technical documentation and reference designs
  • Training and certification programs for our technical staff

Our partnership extends beyond simple distribution - we work closely with Fuji Electric to understand market needs and develop innovative solutions for our customers.
