New 7th Generation X-Series IGBT Module Launch

October 15, 2024 By LiTong Technical Team Company News

Fuji Electric has announced the availability of their latest 7th Generation X-Series IGBT modules, representing a significant advancement in power semiconductor technology. These new modules offer enhanced efficiency and reliability for a wide range of industrial applications.

Key Improvements in the 7th Generation

The 7th Generation X-Series builds upon the success of previous generations with several key improvements:

  • Approximately 20% reduction in switching losses compared to the 6th Generation
  • 10% reduction in conduction losses for improved overall efficiency
  • Enhanced short-circuit withstanding capability for improved system reliability
  • Optimized for higher switching frequencies up to 25kHz
  • Improved temperature cycling performance

Applications and Benefits

These new IGBT modules are particularly well-suited for applications that demand high efficiency and reliability:

  • Industrial motor drives requiring precise control and minimal energy loss
  • Solar inverters where every percentage point of efficiency matters
  • Uninterruptible power supplies (UPS) needing high reliability
  • Electric vehicle charging systems, including fast charging infrastructure

"We're excited to offer our customers access to this cutting-edge technology," said a spokesperson from LiTong Group. "The 7th Generation X-Series represents a significant advancement in power semiconductor performance, and we're committed to providing comprehensive support for our customers implementing these solutions."

Availability and Support

The new 7th Generation X-Series IGBT modules are now available through LiTong Group's global distribution network. Our technical team is prepared to provide design support, thermal analysis, and application guidance to help customers integrate these advanced modules into their systems.

For more information about the 7th Generation X-Series or to request samples, contact our technical support team.
